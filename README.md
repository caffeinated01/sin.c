## sin.c
```
                                                   *****
                                               ****     ****
                                             **             **
*                                          **                 **
 **                                       *                     *
   *                                    **                       **
    **                                **                           *                                 **
      *                              *                              **                              *
       **                           *                                 *                           **
         *                        **                                   **                        *
          **                    **                                       **                    **
            **                **                                           *                 **
              **            **                                              ***            **
                ***      ***                                                   ***      ***
                   ******                                                         ******
```
a simple program that displays a sin wave within your terminal.\
run `gcc sin.c -o sin` to build the executable. \
add a character behind like `./sin '.'` to use `.` instead of the default character which is `*`.
